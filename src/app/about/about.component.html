ABOUT ME

Experience the captivating world of Lesego Matlala, a self-taught South African artist hailing from Limpopo. With an unwavering dedication to hyper-realism, Lesego has spent the past five years masterfully creating breathtaking drawings on white/cream paper using graphite and charcoal. Drawing inspiration from life's journey, his art evokes profound emotions and forges a profound connection with viewers. Guided by principles of patience, practice, and persistence, Lesego's works are a testament to his unwavering pursuit of artistic perfection. Immerse yourself in his mesmerizing creations and witness the passion that flows from his pencils onto the page.
